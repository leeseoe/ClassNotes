## 6. Sort, Cut, and Paste Text Files 
```console
sort music.data
cut -d: -f2-4,6 music.data
sort -t: -o music.new music.data
sort -t: -k 4 music.data > music.sorted 
paste list1 list2 
paste -s list2
```
